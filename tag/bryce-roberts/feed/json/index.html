Bryce Roberts on the temptations that distract from keeping an experiment simple and focused:

\n

\nFor now, we\u2019ve been very conscious not to use any of those exciting terms to describe what we\u2019re doing. And we\u2019re trying our hardest to keep the spirit of the experiment as simple and no-frills as possible.

\n

But the temptations to do \u201cmore\u201d are many.

\n

As with any product, there will be plenty of opportunities to tweak, add or enhance things down the road. But for now the focus is in on staying true to our intent and keeping it simple for anyone evaluating to assess.

\n

Which is much harder to do than it sounds.

\n

It\u2019s so tempting to do, and say yes to, so. much. more.

\n

But not now.

\n

Now, we\u2019re saying no to those many temptations to do more.\n
