Fred Wilson laments the “Silicon Alley” labeling for NYC:

\n

\nIt\u2019s no secret that I HATE the term Silicon Alley. It\u2019s a wannabe term if there ever was one. NYC\u2019s tech community doesn\u2019t want to be Silicon Valley. If they wanted that, they\u2019d move there.\n

\n

\n
