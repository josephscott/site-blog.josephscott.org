Yesterday Skype launched for the PS Vita, which is pretty cool. It is just seven years late is all.

\n

\n

\nSo VOIP on a PSP. Hmmmm, what would sort of software would you actually use to do such a thing? Then another idea came to mind, Skype. If you could port Skype to the PSP then you\u2019d not only be able to VOIP with your PSP, you\u2019d get all of the other things Skype can do. \n
