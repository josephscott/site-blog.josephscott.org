I spent the last week in San Francisco for work and that was my first time flying on JetBlue. I’ve heard plenty of good things about JetBlue and now I’m a fan. There are the obvious things: TV for every seat, free head phones, normal head phone jack, etc. But for me there is one feature above all, I can walk down the isle of the plane without having to hunch over! According to the stats for the Airbus A320 the cabin height is 7 feet 1 inch. I’m not sure if that is the actual head room in the isle, but it was higher than the top of my head and that is what counts in this case.

\n

I’m 6 foot 4 inches tall so I’m used to things not really being designed with my size in mind, which made my experience on JetBlue so pleasantly surprising. I was in the first 11 rows of the plane as well, which have a little bit extra leg room. If I have a choice in the future I’ll be selecting JetBlue.
